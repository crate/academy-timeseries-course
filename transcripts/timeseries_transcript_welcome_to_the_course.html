Welcome to the Time Series course here at the CrateDB Academy.  This course provides a comprehensive overview of working with time series data in CrateDB.  We’ll cover some time series basics, look at some of the complexities associated with real world time series data sets then move on to demonstrate how to ingest, manage, query and visualize time series data in CrateDB. 

This course consists of a series of video lessons, each with subtitles and a transcript.  Many lessons have additional resources: these may be links to relevant parts of the CrateDB documentation, Jupyter notebooks or other resources on GitHub including articles for you to read.  We encourage you to explore these resources and try out the hands-on exercises. 

The assessment for this course is an exam which you can access after completing all the lessons.  The exam consists of a series of multiple-choice questions about the course content.  There’s no time limit and you can refer to the course materials and additional resources whilst taking the exam.  If you score 75% or higher, you’ll earn a Certificate of Completion to share on your LinkedIn profile and social media profiles!  Don’t worry if you don’t pass first time, you can always try again straight away. 

If you have questions about the course, please join our CrateDB Community and post them in the Academy category of our forums.  We’re here to help you! 

We’re excited to share this course with you and hope that you find it useful and enjoyable.
